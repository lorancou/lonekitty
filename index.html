Lone Kitty

Lone Kitty is having bad dreams. Help him to have good dreams again with your keyboard arrow keys.

A Lumdum Dare #22 compo entry. This is Javascript/HTML5/canvas stuff, so normally working with a recent version of any modern browser (Firefox, Chrome...). You have a slight chance to play with IE too, thanks to excanvas. Depending on your browser, sound effects might play, or not, or glitch. Turns out HTML5 audio support is not real good for the moment.

Sources on github. Feel free to comment and rate here (rating is for Ludum Dare participants only).
