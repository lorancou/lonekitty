Lone Kitty

Lone Kitty is having bad dreams. Help him to have good dreams again with your keyboard arrow keys.

A Lumdum Dare #22 compo entry. This is Javascript/HTML5/canvas stuff, so normally working with a recent version of any good browser (if it doesn't with yours, please tell me). Sound effects might play, or not, or glitch. Turns out proper browser support for HTML5 audio isn't completely there, yet.

Sources on github. Feel free to comment and rate here (rating is for Ludum Dare participants only).
